Artist's Statement

This piece was created to touch on the aspect of one's life path.
However, instead of being super literal and following the life path
of a human, I decided to go about it with a lighter tone and have
the audience follow the path of a monarch caterpillar. This is a short
and sweet browser-link "game" that references the butterfly effect. This
piece is unfinished currently but will nonetheless get the point across as
to how our choices can drastically change the course of one's life. Let the
small adventure begin!
