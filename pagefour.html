    • YOU ARE DEAD

    While you were sunbathing, a wasp spotted you and attacked. Wasps use
    caterpillars as a host for their larvae where they will lay their eggs
    inside the caterpillar. When the eggs hatch, the larvae will consume the
    caterpillar from the inside out while the caterpillar is still alive. It's
    pretty metal but also not a great way to go. You should have found a safe
    place to hide.
